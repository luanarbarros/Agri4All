What is Agri4All?

A free, affordable and inclusive AI-based mobile app made for small and medium farmers that automatically diagnoses crop pests and provides them economic benefits. The benefits of our solution includes:

  1. The recognition of crop pests by AI reduces the time to diagnosis and the risk of error
  2. Offers an end-to-end process: diagnosis + treatment + causes + prevention + sales. Our solution also connects the farmer with his end customer
  3. Our tools aim to teach and empower our users in the long term. Also is completely accessible for the illiterate

Why this project is so important?

Find out the reasons that led us to create this solution

Accessible for illiterate

70% of family farmers do not have completed elementary school and 20% of them are illiterate, and therefore do not have access to current solutions. Our app has an interface designed for this audience.

Support via smartphone

80% of family farmers do not receive any technological support, this lack of access makes it difficult to detect pests. On the other hand, 61% of the family farmers have a smartphone. We can provide a strong analysis tool via smartphone.

Access to healthier food

Helps to reduce the current cost of $14.7 billion of agricultural pests for the Brazilian economy. As a result, consumers can have access to healthier foods and also generate income to family farmers, that can be found using our app.
